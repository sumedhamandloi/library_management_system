Business Analysis: Best Practices for Success

Steven P. Blais

Business Analysis is a comprehensive guide that provides best practices and techniques for successful business analysis. The book covers the essential skills and knowledge needed to analyze business requirements, identify solutions, and facilitate change within organizations.

This book is designed for business analysts, project managers, and students studying business administration or information systems. It includes practical examples, case studies, and methodologies that help readers understand how to effectively analyze business needs and recommend solutions that align with organizational goals.
