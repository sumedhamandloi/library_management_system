Introduction to Algorithms

Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein

Introduction to Algorithms is a comprehensive textbook on algorithms and data structures that is widely used in computer science education. The book covers a broad range of algorithms in depth, making it an essential resource for students and professionals in the field.

This fourth edition has been thoroughly updated and includes new material on topics such as multithreaded algorithms, dynamic programming, and graph algorithms. The book provides a thorough introduction to the mathematical analysis of algorithms and their design, with clear explanations and numerous examples.
