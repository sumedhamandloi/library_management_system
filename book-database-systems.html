Database Systems: Design, Implementation, and Management

Carlos Coronel, Steven Morris

Database Systems is a comprehensive textbook that provides a solid foundation in database design and implementation. The book covers fundamental database concepts, data modeling, SQL, database administration, and advanced topics in database management.

This book is designed for students and professionals who need to understand how to design, implement, and manage database systems effectively. It includes practical examples, case studies, and exercises that help readers apply database concepts in real-world scenarios. Essential for computer science and information technology students.
